# THE EXCELLENT MAN — THE EPIGRAPH BANK
## Vetted quotes for all 52 chapters

**Legend:**
✓ = verified source, safe to print with citation
~ = widely attributed but original source uncertain — print as "attributed to," or replace
Public-domain sources (pre-1928 or ancient) can be quoted freely. Modern quotes kept brief (standard epigraph use).

**House rule (fits the book's ethic):** we never print a misattributed quote. Precision in small things — even in who said what — is the whole message. Where a beloved quote is dubious, we either mark it honestly or use the verified original it was paraphrased from.
